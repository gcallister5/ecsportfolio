Skiing

Inspiration

Andreas Fransson

The reason Andreas Fransson is one of my skiing inspirations is because he is a freestyle skiier that would ski through places like the patagonian backcountry and he was fearless while skiing it.

Accomplishments

While I've never skiied as a ski team I ski pretty regularly with friends, family, or just by myself. I like to ski past the expert gates and explore new and challenging terrain such as moguls, gullies, or chutes. My favorite places to ski are Alta and Brighton.
